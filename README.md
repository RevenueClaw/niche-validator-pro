# 🔍 Niche Validator Pro

**Validate your business idea in 60 seconds. No login. No BS. Works offline.**

→ **[Try it free](https://revenueclaw.github.io/niche-validator-pro/)**

---

## What it does

Plug in your niche idea, answer 4 quick sliders, and get:

- **0–100 viability score** across demand, competition, reach, and expertise
- **Instant verdict** — go, proceed with caution, or pivot
- **5 tailored product ideas** with real price points for your category

No accounts. No SaaS. No tracking. Just a single HTML file that runs in your browser.
